<p>I&#8217;m currently on holiday in Lanzarote, which is one of the Canary Islands. It&#8217;s technically part of Spain, but it&#8217;s closer to the western coast of Africa.</p>
<p>We spent all of today travelling around the south side of the island, and ended in a place called El Golfo, which has some very rocky beaches. It resulted in some interesting photos that I thought would look good as mobile wallpapers, so I spent some extra time seeing what else would look good, and I came up with 12 different options!</p>
